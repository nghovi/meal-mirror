Privacy Policy

Meal Mirror

This Privacy Policy explains how Meal Mirror collects, uses, shares, retains, and protects information when you use the app to capture meal photos, estimate calories, and review your diet over time by day, week, month, and year.

Effective date: April 1, 2026 URL: ankieng.truongdiem.online/privacy

At a glance

  • Developer contact support@truongdiem.online
  • App purpose Meal logging and diet review over time
  • Data used Meal photos, meal-related analysis, and technical request data
  • Third party OpenAI processes meal images to return analysis
  • User experience Intended to help review eating patterns by day, week, month, and year

1. Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to the Meal Mirror mobile app and to the privacy policy page published at ankieng.truongdiem.online/privacy. The app is designed to help users capture photos of meals such as breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks so AI can review diet patterns over time.

2. Information we collect

When you choose or capture a meal image in the app, the selected image is sent for analysis so the app can generate calorie and diet-related output. Depending on the product version, the service may also process meal labels or time context such as breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, and dates associated with entries so the app can organize dietary reviews over time. The app may also process limited technical information required for network requests and app operation, such as IP address, device or browser request metadata, and error or diagnostic data generated while the service is running.

3. How we use information

We use uploaded meal images and related information to provide calorie estimation, summarize eating patterns, support diet review by day, week, month, and year, return analysis results in the app, monitor service reliability, and improve the quality of the product.

4. Third-party processing

Meal images are transmitted to OpenAI so the app can analyze image content and generate calorie and diet-related output. Hosting or infrastructure providers may also process technical request data as part of delivering the service. Your use of the app means you understand this processing is required for the core feature to work.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with service providers involved in operating the calorie analysis feature, hosting the site, securing the service, or complying with lawful obligations.

6. Data retention

Uploaded data is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to deliver the service, support meal history and diet review features, troubleshoot issues, and meet legal or operational requirements. Retention periods may change as the product evolves.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Please avoid uploading sensitive images that you do not want processed by third-party AI services.

8. Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time and choose not to upload images. You may also request deletion of submitted data that can reasonably be identified and located by contacting the developer at support@truongdiem.online. If future versions add richer account, logging, or analytics controls, this page will be updated to explain those options.

9. Children's privacy

The app is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to operate the image analysis feature. If you believe inappropriate information has been submitted, please contact us so we can review it.

10. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published at this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

Developer and privacy contact: support@truongdiem.online
